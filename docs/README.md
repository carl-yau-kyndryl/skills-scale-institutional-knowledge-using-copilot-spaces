# Overview of Project Management Processes at OctoAcme

At OctoAcme, we utilize a variety of project management processes to ensure efficiency and effectiveness in our operations. Our methodologies are designed to foster collaboration, streamline tasks, and optimize resource allocation.

## Key Processes:
1. **Agile Methodology** - Emphasizes iterative development and collaboration.
   - More details can be found in the Agile Process Document.
2. **Waterfall Approach** - A linear approach suitable for projects with clear requirements.
   - Refer to the Waterfall Process Document.
3. **Scrum Framework** - A subset of Agile, focusing on managing tasks within a team-based development environment. 
   - For more information, see the Scrum Process Document.
